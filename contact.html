About the brand

Pretty routines are better when they are not built on bad information.

Stylishandhealthy is for readers who like beauty, skincare, wellness, and self-improvement, but do not want to be manipulated by every viral claim online.

The point

This is not anti-beauty. It is anti-nonsense.

I started Stylishandhealthy because beauty content often acts like you have two options: blindly trust trends or become cynical about everything. The better option is to stay curious, check the claim, and make a decision that fits your skin, budget, and actual life.

The tone is soft, but the standard is not. If a product is overhyped, I want to say that. If a boring routine step works, I want to make it look good enough that people actually stick with it.
