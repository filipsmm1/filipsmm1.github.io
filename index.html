Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which cookies this website uses, and how you can manage them. This site is operated by Filip Lovric trading as FilipSocial.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, phone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work correctly, work more efficiently, and to provide information to the owners of the site. Cookies do not give anyone access to your device or any personal information about you other than the data you choose to share. Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings.

Why Does This Website Use Cookies?

This website uses cookies for the following reasons. First, to make the site work correctly. Certain features, including the payment form powered by Stripe, require cookies to function. These are called strictly necessary cookies and are used regardless of your cookie preferences. Second, to remember your preferences. For example, we use a cookie to remember whether you prefer the dark or light mode, and whether you have already accepted or declined our cookie notice. Third, to understand how visitors use the site. If you accept analytics cookies, we may use tools that track which pages are visited, how long visitors stay, and where they came from. This helps us improve the site over time. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics cookies.

Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly necessary cookies are required for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They include cookies set by Stripe for secure payment processing, and a cookie that stores your theme preference (dark or light mode). These do not collect any personal data and are always active.

Analytics cookies are only placed if you click Accept on our cookie banner. These help us understand which pages are most popular and how visitors navigate the site. They do not identify you personally.

Preference cookies store choices you make on the site, such as your theme preference and your cookie consent decision. These are stored locally in your browser.

Third-Party Cookies

When you use our payment form, Stripe may set its own cookies on your device to enable secure payment processing and fraud prevention. These cookies are governed by Stripe's own privacy policy, which you can read at stripe.com/privacy.

How to Manage Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways. You can use the Accept or Decline buttons on our cookie banner when you first visit the site. You can also clear cookies at any time through your browser settings. The steps vary by browser but are usually found under Settings, Privacy, or History. Please note that if you decline or delete cookies, some features of the site may not work as expected, including the payment form.

Your Consent

By clicking Accept on our cookie banner, you consent to the use of analytics and preference cookies as described above. Strictly necessary cookies are always active as they are required for the site to function. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.
